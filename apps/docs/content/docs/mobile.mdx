Terragon works seamlessly on mobile devices in the browser or as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

For the best experience, add Terragon to your home screen for quick access:

### iOS

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Navigate to [terragonlabs.com](https://terragonlabs.com) in your mobile browser
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Tap the <Share className="inline h-4 w-4" /> button
  </Step>
  <Step>Select `Add to Home Screen`</Step>
</Steps>

### Android

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Navigate to [terragonlabs.com](https://terragonlabs.com) in your mobile browser
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Tap the <EllipsisVertical className="inline h-4 w-4" /> menu
  </Step>
  <Step>Select `Add to Home screen`</Step>
  <Step>Confirm installation</Step>
</Steps>
