**Rover** is a developer platform that lets you run **AI coding agents** in the cloud. Whether you're at your browser, in your terminal, or on your phone, Rover works wherever you are. Offload coding tasks and come back to pull requests ready to review.

At a high level, Rover works like this:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Describe your task**

    Select your GitHub repo and describe your task

  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Agent works on your task**

    Your repo is cloned to a cloud sandbox and an agent starts working on your task

  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Review & Merge**

    Once the agent is done, it automatically opens a pull request for you to review

  </Step>
</Steps>

### Why Use Rover?

<CircleCheckBig className="inline h-4 w-4 mr-1 text-primary" /> **Use all the best agents:** Choose from Claude Code, OpenAI Codex, Amp.

<CircleCheckBig className="inline h-4 w-4 mr-1 text-primary" /> **Work from anywhere:** Rover works from your phone and lets you work wherever you are.

<CircleCheckBig className="inline h-4 w-4 mr-1 text-primary" /> **Work on multiple tasks without conflicts:** Each agent runs in an isolated environment.

<CircleCheckBig className="inline h-4 w-4 mr-1 text-primary" /> **Works with the tools you already use:** Seamlessly delegate work from GitHub, Slack and more.

<CircleCheckBig className="inline h-4 w-4 mr-1 text-primary" /> **Take control locally:** Pull tasks to your local environment when they need your attention.

<CircleCheckBig className="inline h-4 w-4 mr-1 text-primary" /> **Offload repetitive tasks:** Recurring tasks or event-triggered workflows let you focus on what matters.
